ABOUT US

WHO WE ARE

This ART GALLERY has been established in 2000 by fine arts students, with the aim of enhancing and valorising the cultural and artistic heritage of our society.
The approaches of Dreams and Brushstrokes ART GALLERY are the acquisition of new works of art, available to the public and the prominent cultural institutions in the area, and the implementation of projects for the development, strengthening and efficiency, of the entire system.

VISION

Our CONTEMPORARY ARTS inspires local and regional artists through active involvement with the international contemporary art community.Our ART GALLERY strives to be viewed as a credible and internationally respected art establishment; while empowering artists with the facilities, environment and experience to excel in their medium and enrich their lives.

MISSION

Our CONTEMPORARY ARTS creates and shares globally influenced, culturally diverse, and technologically advanced arts programing.

CORE VALUES


WELCOMING

To breakdown existing and perceived barriers surrounding contemporary art.

ENTREPRENEURIAL

To be an established, valuable organization that takes risks and inspires change.

INCLUSIVE

To be a vehicle for respectful dialogue, diverse representation, and unbiased opportunity.

EXCELLENCE

To foster a contemporary art experience focused on excellence and accessibility.
